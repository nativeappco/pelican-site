What do product FAQs achieve?
A Product FAQ block on your product page lets shoppers get quick answers to the stuff that normally lives in a support ticket: sizing, materials, care, shipping, compatibility.

No external database

Every FAQ is stored as a native Shopify metaobject entry, assigned to products with a metafield. You own the data, Pelican just helps you generate and optimize it.

Write once, reuse everywhere

One FAQ can be reused across as many products as you like — no copy-pasting, no drift between products.

Survives theme changes

Because it's not tied to one theme's code, your FAQs stay put when you re-theme or redesign.

Why it matters more than ever

Two audiences read your FAQs now: people, and their AI assistants.

Good FAQs aren't just customer service anymore — they're how your products get recommended by ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude & Google's AI Overviews.

For customers

Fewer tickets, less friction.

Answering the obvious questions upfront reduces support tickets and removes friction right before checkout — where it counts most.

For AI

The format LLMs are built to read.

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Google's AI Overviews are increasingly the first place shoppers ask "does this run small" or "is this machine washable." Product Pelican automatically outputs FAQPage structured data (JSON-LD) alongside every FAQ block — exactly the format AI crawlers and LLMs are built to read.

01 · Workflow

How Pelican does it.

Suggest, reuse, edit, import — all in bulk.
STEP 01
Suggest.

Select a product, hit Suggest FAQs, and Pelican drafts a set of questions and answers straight from that product's actual details. Accept the ones you want, discard the rest, or hit Accept All to take the whole batch.

STEP 02
Reuse.

Selling the same product in ten colours? Write the sizing FAQ once and use Connect Existing to assign it everywhere. No copy-pasting, no drift.

STEP 03
Edit.

Click any FAQ to tweak the wording. Drag to reorder. Delete what you don't need. Full control over every question.

STEP 04
Import.

Already have FAQ content in a spreadsheet? Import FAQs from CSV in one go — product_id, question, answer.

STEP 05
Find gaps.

Filter your catalogue by "No FAQs" to see exactly which products still need attention, or by Active / Draft / Archived to focus your effort.

  1. Filter the sidebar to No FAQs
  2. Select a product, click Suggest FAQs
  3. Click Accept All (or pick your favourites)
  4. Done — FAQs are live on that product
Under a minute per product.
Simple Theme Block

Control how it looks.

Add the Product FAQs app block to your product template in the Shopify theme editor. It pulls in whatever FAQs are assigned to that product and displays them as an accordion or text.

Use it out of the box for a clean, on-brand accordion that's already doing the AI/SEO heavy lifting in the background — or take full control of the markup and styling if your theme needs something specific.

Raw HTML output hands your theme plain h3/p tags with no styling of Pelican's own — your theme's CSS takes over completely.
